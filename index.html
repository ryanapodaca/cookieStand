About Us

Hello, we are a family owned business that makes and distributes Salmon Cookies! Ours is a long and prosperous generational tradition, which started in 1888, the year our great great grandpa won the annual cookie contest in Irelamnd with his now infamous salmon cookies. It was a contentous contest that lives in the memory of everyone that fateful day, and its results have brought our family a way to live well with our storied past.
